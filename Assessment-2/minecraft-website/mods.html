Popular Minecraft Mods

Optifine

A pioneer in Minecraft performance optimization, Optifine is one of the most popular mods. It improves graphics and boosts FPS.

Just Enough Items (JEI)

Lets you view item recipes and usages easily in-game.

Sodium

A powerful optimization mod that enhances performance, especially on lower-end systems. It has become a popular alternative to Optifine and recent tests show that Sodium is faster.

Simple Voice Chat

It adds in-game voice communication, which enhances multiplayer experience.

JourneyMap

This allows players to mark coordinates and creates real-time waypoints and maps in-game which helps players for navigation and exploration.
